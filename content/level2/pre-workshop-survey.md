## Core Insight

The Pre-Workshop Survey is an intake instrument deployed before every Meaningful AI engagement to customize the experience to the specific audience. It captures current AI familiarity levels, specific pain points, role-specific challenges, organizational priorities, and individual expectations. This data drives the customization of workshop content, exercise design, and follow-up recommendations — ensuring that every session addresses the actual needs of the room, not generic AI talking points.

## Context

Generic AI workshops fail because they talk about AI in the abstract. A CFO cares about different things than a CHRO, who cares about different things than a COO. The Pre-Workshop Survey was developed to solve this mismatch by capturing audience-specific intelligence before the session. It also serves a secondary purpose: it signals to participants that Meaningful AI takes their specific situation seriously, building trust and engagement before the workshop even begins.

## Detail

### Survey Components

**AI Familiarity Assessment:** Where does each participant currently sit on the AI adoption spectrum? Are they complete beginners, casual users, or power users? This determines the starting complexity level.

**Pain Point Identification:** What specific problems keep participants up at night? What tasks consume disproportionate time? Where do they see opportunity? These real-world problems become the foundation for workshop exercises.

**Role-Specific Context:** What decisions does each participant make regularly? What data do they rely on? What workflows define their daily work? This enables hyper-relevant exercise design.

**Organizational Alignment:** What are the company's strategic priorities? Where has AI been discussed at the leadership level? What concerns or resistance exist? This ensures the workshop aligns with (and advances) organizational strategy.

**Expectations and Concerns:** What do participants hope to get from the workshop? What are they worried about? This allows facilitators to proactively address concerns and deliver on expectations.

### How Survey Data Drives Customization

For a pharmaceutical audience, the survey might reveal that regulatory compliance is the top concern. The workshop would then lead with compliance-friendly AI use cases and address regulatory guardrails early.

For a manufacturing audience, the survey might surface supply chain optimization and demand forecasting as priorities, leading to exercises built around production data analysis.

For a financial services audience, the survey might highlight risk management and customer personalization, shaping exercises around those themes.

## Application Guide

1. **Deploy:** Send the survey 2-3 weeks before the engagement.
2. **Analyze:** Cluster responses to identify common themes, outliers, and priority areas.
3. **Customize:** Modify workshop content, exercises, and examples based on survey findings.
4. **Reference:** During the workshop, explicitly reference survey themes to show responsiveness.
5. **Follow Up:** Use survey data to personalize post-workshop recommendations.

## Key Takeaways

- Pre-Workshop Surveys transform generic AI sessions into customized, relevant experiences
- The survey captures familiarity levels, pain points, role context, organizational priorities, and expectations
- Survey data directly drives exercise design and content customization
- The survey itself builds trust and engagement before the session begins
- Serves as a diagnostic tool that feeds into broader engagement planning
