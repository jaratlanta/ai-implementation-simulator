## Core Insight

Meaningful AI's Executive AI Immersion Workshop is a hands-on, keyboards-open education experience designed for senior leadership. Unlike typical AI presentations where executives passively watch demos, participants actively use AI tools on problems relevant to their specific roles and industries. The workshop is customized per audience using a pre-workshop survey and serves as both an education tool and a gateway engagement for deeper AI transformation work.

## Context

Executive AI literacy is the single biggest bottleneck in enterprise AI adoption. Leaders can't make informed decisions about AI strategy if they've never personally used the tools. The Immersion Workshop was developed to solve this: get executives hands-on with AI, in a structured environment, working on their actual business problems. This isn't a tech demo — it's an experiential learning session that transforms skeptics into champions and gives leaders the confidence to authorize larger AI investments.

## Detail

### Workshop Design Principles

**Keyboards Open:** Every participant has a laptop open and is actively using AI tools. No passive observation. The learning is experiential.

**Customized Per Audience:** A workshop for pharmaceutical VP-level leaders (like a pharmaceutical company engagement for 300+ VPs) looks different from a workshop for community bank executives. The use cases, examples, prompts, and exercises are tailored to the audience's industry, role, and specific challenges.

**Pre-Workshop Survey:** Before the workshop, participants complete a survey that captures their current AI familiarity, specific pain points, role-specific challenges, and expectations. This data is used to customize the session content, ensuring every exercise is immediately relevant.

**Progressive Complexity:** Start with simple, confidence-building exercises (summarize this document, draft this email) and progress to more complex scenarios (analyze this dataset, build a workflow). This mirrors the Gear 1 journey from basic usage to confident integration.

**Real Problems, Not Toy Examples:** Exercises are built around actual business scenarios from the audience's industry. A manufacturing workshop might involve analyzing production data. A financial services workshop might involve regulatory document review.

### Scale Capability

The methodology scales from intimate C-suite sessions (5-10 people) to large leadership events (300+ people). The core design is maintained at scale through breakout sessions, facilitated small groups, and role-specific exercise tracks.

### Outcomes

- Leaders gain firsthand experience with AI capabilities and limitations
- Common fears and misconceptions are addressed through direct experience
- Organizations leave with a shared vocabulary and baseline understanding
- Champions are identified for deeper engagement
- The workshop naturally surfaces high-value use cases for follow-up work

## Application Guide

1. **Survey:** Deploy pre-workshop survey 2+ weeks before the session.
2. **Customize:** Build exercises around the audience's specific industry, roles, and pain points.
3. **Deliver:** Facilitate a hands-on session with progressive complexity.
4. **Capture:** Document the use cases, questions, and opportunities that emerge during the session.
5. **Follow Up:** Convert workshop energy into concrete next steps (Innovation Sprints, Gear 2 engagements, etc.).

## Key Takeaways

- Hands-on, keyboards-open format — executives use AI, not just watch it
- Customized per audience using pre-workshop survey data
- Scales from small executive sessions to 300+ person events
- Serves as gateway engagement for larger AI transformation work
- Transforms AI skeptics into informed champions
