## Core Insight

Meaningful AI structures engagements in tiers that match client readiness and budget, from lightweight education to comprehensive transformation. The key innovation is that each tier delivers standalone value while naturally creating appetite for the next tier. An Executive Immersion Workshop ($1K/person for full-day) leads to an Innovation Sprint ($21K-$45K for 4-5 weeks), which leads to custom software development, which leads to ongoing advisory retainers. The pricing is deliberately positioned at a fraction of traditional consulting firms — sometimes 10x less — creating a "loss leader" dynamic that builds long-term partnerships.

## Context

Meaningful AI competes against both large consulting firms (McKinsey, Deloitte) and narrow AI tool vendors. The tiered model creates a middle path that's more affordable than consulting firms and more strategic than tool vendors. A manufacturing client engagement demonstrates the tier structure: a Strategic Accelerator ($21K) validates use cases and builds demonstrations for the board, while the Full Innovation Launch ($45K) produces functional prototypes with extensible architecture, ROI models, change management briefs, and 90-day execution roadmaps. The Accel-KKR pricing shows the PE-focused tiers: $55K Activate and $95K Amplify.

## Detail

### The Tier Structure

**Tier 0: Free Workshop / Webinar**
Free educational events (like the April 15th ATDC workshop) serve as top-of-funnel activities. They build relationships, demonstrate expertise, and surface potential clients. No cost to the client. Meaningful AI invests in venue, catering, and preparation.

**Tier 1: Executive Immersion Workshop ($1K-$5K/person)**
Hands-on, customized workshop ranging from 1.5-hour executive sessions to full-day immersions. Delivers immediate education value while surfacing high-value use cases for follow-up.

**Tier 2: Strategic Accelerator / Innovation Sprint ($21K-$55K)**
4-5 week bounded engagement producing validated use cases, industry benchmarking, and working demonstrations. The Strategic Accelerator program at $21K validates and benchmarks. The Accel-KKR "Activate" at $55K includes training, change management infrastructure, and behavioral economics adoption methodology.

**Tier 3: Full Innovation Launch / Amplify ($45K-$95K)**
Comprehensive engagement producing functional prototypes with extensible architecture, quantified ROI models, change management briefs, and 90-day execution roadmaps. Designed to hand off systems the client can extend and operate.

**Tier 4: Monthly Retainer / Fractional CAIO**
Ongoing advisory and implementation support. Ranges from lightweight monthly advisory to embedded expert squad. Recurring revenue model for Meaningful AI with continuous value delivery for the client.

### Pricing Philosophy

**10x Less than Consulting Firms:** Deliberate positioning. A multi-agent LLM research system running for 1-2 hours delivers the equivalent of "13 McKinsey experts" at a fraction of the cost.

**Loss Leader on Entry:** Early engagements are priced to build relationships, not maximize margin. The long-term value of a deep client partnership far exceeds the revenue from any single engagement.

**Extensible Architecture as Differentiator:** Every tier produces deliverables the client can extend after the engagement, ensuring value persists beyond the contract.

### Board Retreat as Engagement Milestone

A key strategic insight from a manufacturing client engagement: position the engagement so "the board retreat becomes a milestone in work that's already underway, not the starting line." This reframes the client relationship from vendor engagement to ongoing strategic partnership.

## Application Guide

1. **Qualify Entry Point:** Determine which tier matches the client's current readiness and budget.
2. **Deliver Standalone Value:** Ensure each tier delivers complete value regardless of whether the client progresses.
3. **Surface Next Opportunities:** Each tier should naturally reveal the value of the next tier.
4. **Price for Relationship:** Optimize for long-term partnership value, not single-engagement margin.
5. **Design for Extension:** All deliverables should be usable and extensible after the engagement ends.

## Key Takeaways

- Engagement tiers range from free workshops to $95K comprehensive programs
- Each tier delivers standalone value while creating appetite for the next
- Pricing at 10x less than traditional consulting firms builds competitive advantage
- Loss leader entry pricing builds long-term partnerships worth multiples of initial engagement
- Architecture extensibility ensures value persists beyond the contract
